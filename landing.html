Installation Instructions
Option 1: Install PracticeTrack (Recommended)

For the best experience, install PracticeTrack on your device:

On iPhone/iPad:

  1. Open Safari and visit this page
  2. Tap Share icon at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and select "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top right corner

On Android:

  1. Open Chrome and visit this page
  2. Tap the menu (three dots) in the top right
  3. Select "Add to Home screen"
  4. Confirm by tapping "Add"
Option 2: Use in Browser

PracticeTrack works in any modern browser. Simply click "Use in Browser" above to get started immediately.
