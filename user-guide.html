Welcome to PracticeTrack

PracticeTrack is your personal practice companion, designed to help you track and improve your musical practice sessions. This guide will help you get started with all the features available in the app.

Quick Start Guide

1

Set Up Your Profile

Go to Settings and configure your preferences:

  • Choose your primary instrument
  • Set your lesson day and time
  • Select your preferred theme (light/dark)
2

Add Practice Categories

Visit the Resources page to:

  • Browse available practice categories
  • Add categories to your practice list
  • Create custom categories if needed
3

Start Practicing

Use the Timer page to:

  • Select a practice category
  • Start your practice session
  • Add notes about your progress
  • Save your session when done

Timer Page

The timer is your main practice tool. Here's how to use it:

  1. Select a practice category from the dropdown menu
  2. Click "Start" to begin your practice session
  3. Use "Pause" to take a break and "Resume" to continue
  4. Click "Stop" to end the session
  5. Add notes about your practice session
  6. Click "Save Session" to record your practice time

Quick Tip

You can use the spacebar to start/pause the timer when you're not typing in the notes field.

Resources Page

The Resources page helps you organize your practice materials:

  • Browse and search practice categories
  • View detailed information about each category
  • Add categories to your practice list
  • Access helpful practice tips and resources

Note

Categories added in the Resources page will be available in the Timer page dropdown.

Settings Page

Customize your practice experience in the Settings page:

  • Manage your practice categories
  • Set your lesson day and time
  • Choose your primary instrument
  • Toggle between light and dark theme
  • Export or import your practice data
  • Clear all data if needed

Pro Tip

You can create custom practice categories in the Settings page to match your specific needs.

Need Help?

If you need assistance or have questions:

  • Check the Resources page for practice categories and tips
  • Review your practice history in the Timer page
  • Customize your experience in the Settings page
