Welcome to PracticeTrack

PracticeTrack is your personal practice companion, designed to help you track and improve your musical practice sessions. This guide will help you get started with all the features available in the app.

Getting Started

To begin using PracticeTrack:

  1. Select your instrument(s) in the settings menu
  2. Choose your preferred practice categories
  3. Set up your practice goals (optional)

Quick Tip

You can access settings at any time by clicking the settings icon in the top-right corner of the app.

Using the Timer

The timer is your main practice tool. Here's how to use it:

  1. Select a practice category from the dropdown menu
  2. Click "Start" to begin your practice session
  3. Use "Stop" to pause and "Reset" to start over
  4. When finished, click "Save Session" to record your practice time

Note

The timer will automatically save your session when you click "Save Session". You can add notes about your practice session before saving.

Tracking Your Progress

PracticeTrack helps you monitor your progress in several ways:

  • View your practice history in the Sessions tab
  • Check your statistics and progress in the Stats tab
  • Track your goals in the Goals tab
  • Add photos, videos, and notes in the Media tab

Practice Categories

PracticeTrack comes with predefined categories for various instruments. You can:

  • Browse categories by instrument family in the Resources tab
  • Add categories to your practice list with one click
  • Create custom categories in the settings menu

Pro Tip

Use the search function in the Resources tab to quickly find specific practice categories.

Need Help?

If you need assistance or have questions:

  • Check the Resources tab for instrument-specific practice categories
  • Review your practice history in the Sessions tab
  • Contact support through the settings menu

Happy practicing!
